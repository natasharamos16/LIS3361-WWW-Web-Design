    • About Us

    Growing up I never knew who to talk to. I always felt that no one really understood how I felt. I turned to writing.
    Writing poetry has saved my life. I am able to write my feelings and it is like writing the story of my life.
    I have loved poetry as a little girl and it has helped build confidence and character. I am able create art and share my stories with others.
    I wanted to create this website for individuals like me to be able to express themselves through writing.
    So I created Love Haiku, a place for those who admire poetry and haikus.

    Our Mission

    To help those in need. If anyone ever feels lonely or has no one to turn to.
    We are here to help comfort those through poetry.

    Our Style

    We are all about peace and love. We welcome anyone that has the passion to write through poetry.
